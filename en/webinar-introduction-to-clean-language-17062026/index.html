What is Clean Language

Clean Language is a set of questions that make it easier to listen in a way that removes noise and our own unconscious biases from communication.

It helps us — through focused attention, presence and listening — understand ourselves, the person we're talking to, groups and teams, the whole organization and, ultimately, society as a whole in the act of communication.

That understanding happens through metaphor as a unit of thought, which sits between the conscious and the unconscious. Every metaphor means something different to different people. Our job is not to interpret, but to ask — and truly hear the answer.

What is Systemic Modelling

Systemic Modelling is an effective way to recognize group patterns. It helps spot moments of unnecessary drama and redirect the group's attention toward curiosity and engagement.

This fosters collaboration and coordinated, results-oriented action. The approach can be applied across a wide range of settings, from boardrooms to classrooms.
